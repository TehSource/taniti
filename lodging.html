Lodging

Stay your way. Options range from an inexpensive hostel to a large four‑star resort. There are many family‑owned hotels and a growing number of bed and breakfasts. All lodging is strictly regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government.

Highlights

  • Budget to luxury choices
  • Family‑owned hotels and B&Bs
